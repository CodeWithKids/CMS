# KPIs and OKRs by Profile

This document describes the **metrics (KPIs)** currently surfaced in CWK Hub per role and suggested **OKRs (Objectives and Key Results)** for each profile. Formal OKRs are not yet stored in the app; this is the single source of truth for product and future implementation.

---

## 1. Admin (Operations)

### KPIs currently in the app
- **People:** Total learners, total organisations (schools / organisations / Miradi), pending account approvals
- **Finance:** Total revenue (from finance account), learners with pending payments, organisations with pending payments
- **Operations:** Session reports, classes, enrollments (via navigation to sub-pages)

### Suggested OKRs
| Objective | Key results |
|-----------|-------------|
| **O1:** Run operations smoothly and keep the organisation solvent | KR1: X% of invoices collected within 30 days; KR2: Zero critical compliance gaps; KR3: All pending approvals resolved within 5 days |
| **O2:** Scale reach and impact | KR1: Y active partner organisations; KR2: Z active learners across programmes; KR3: N sessions delivered per term |

---

## 2. Finance

### KPIs currently in the app
- **Total gross invoiced** (term)
- **Total discounts** (term)
- **Net invoiced** (term)
- **Amount paid** (term)
- **Outstanding** (total balance due)
- **Overdue** (amount and count past due)
- Invoice status breakdown (draft, sent, paid, etc.)
- Pending adjustment requests

### Suggested OKRs
| Objective | Key results |
|-----------|-------------|
| **O1:** Maintain accurate, transparent financial records | KR1: All income/expense data sourced from finance account; KR2: Invoices issued within 5 days of billing event; KR3: < X% outstanding over 60 days |
| **O2:** Support cash flow and planning | KR1: Monthly income vs expense visibility; KR2: Salaries, rent, utilities tracked as monthly expenses; KR3: Clear categorisation (parent income, school, organisation) |

---

## 3. Educator

### KPIs currently in the app
- **Today’s sessions** (count and list)
- **Upcoming sessions** (next 10)
- **Past sessions** (recent 5)
- **My classes** (assigned)
- **Sessions without report** (this week) – reminder count
- **Devices checked out** (from inventory)
- **Badges** (earned)
- **Hours / earnings** (via Educator earnings page)
- **Lesson plan status** per session (draft / ready)
- **Session report status** (submitted / not submitted)
- **Objectives met** (per session report: yes / partially / no)

### Suggested OKRs
| Objective | Key results |
|-----------|-------------|
| **O1:** Deliver quality sessions consistently | KR1: 100% of sessions have a submitted report within 48 hours; KR2: Lesson plan marked “Ready” before session where applicable; KR3: Objectives met ≥ “partially” for X% of sessions |
| **O2:** Grow as a facilitator | KR1: Complete L&D coaching actions; KR2: Average learner feedback ≥ 4/5; KR3: No repeated “challenges” without follow-up |

---

## 4. L&D Manager

### KPIs currently in the app
- **Active tracks** (count of tracks with lesson plan templates)
- **Educators supported** (count)
- **Avg learner feedback** (across feedback responses)
- **Sessions this week** (across all tracks)
- **Alerts:** Sessions with challenges flagged, low engagement reports, templates needing review
- **Learning reports:** By track (sessions, avg feedback, challenges), content health (templates per track)
- **Coaching notes** (count per educator)
- **Session reports** (read-only list and detail)

### Suggested OKRs
| Objective | Key results |
|-----------|-------------|
| **O1:** Improve curriculum and teaching quality | KR1: All active tracks have ≥ N lesson plan templates; KR2: Average learner feedback ≥ 4/5 per track; KR3: Reduce “sessions with challenges” by X% quarter over quarter |
| **O2:** Build educator capability | KR1: Every educator receives ≥ 1 coaching note or observation per term; KR2: Session report completion rate 100%; KR3: Low-engagement sessions reviewed and actioned |
